Beself 隐私政策

更新日期：2026 年 4 月 12 日 · 生效日期：2026 年 4 月 12 日

欢迎使用 Beself（以下简称「本应用」）。我们重视您的个人信息保护，并将依法采取合理安全措施。 请在安装或使用前仔细阅读本政策。

一、适用范围

本政策适用于您在 Android、Windows 等终端安装、使用本应用时，我们对相关个人信息的处理活动。 本应用以网页浏览与本地工具为主；您主动访问的第三方网站或服务，由其各自隐私政策约束。

二、我们处理的信息

  • 设备与网络状态：用于联网、下载与基础稳定性（如网络是否可用），不用于单独识别个人身份。
  • 本地应用数据：设置、书签类数据、缓存、笔记及您导出的文件等，默认保存在本机应用专属目录；除非您自行分享或上传至第三方，我们不会将上述内容上传至开发者自有服务器。
  • 相机、麦克风、位置：仅在系统授权且网页或功能合法请求时使用；您可随时在系统设置中关闭。
  • 通知：用于下载完成、前台任务状态等提示；可在系统设置中管理。
  • 文件与媒体：通过系统文件选择器或您明确授权的路径访问，不超出操作范围扫描无关文件。

三、权限说明（以系统实际提示为准）

  • 网络与网络状态：加载网页、下载与连接检测。
  • 前台服务：合规的后台同步或媒体播放相关任务及状态通知。
  • 相机、录音、音频设置、唤醒锁定：网页或应用内多媒体场景。
  • 存储与媒体读取：保存与读取应用数据与您选择的文件；遵循分区存储与最小必要原则。
  • 位置：在网页或功能需要且您授权时使用。
  • 振动：轻量交互反馈。

四、第三方与内容

第三方网站可能独立收集您的信息。请阅读其政策；我们不对第三方行为承担责任，应用内将尽量遵循平台安全实践（如优先加密传输）。

五、存储与安全

  • 数据主要存于终端，请妥善保管设备。
  • 我们采取合理措施防范未授权访问、泄露、损毁。
  • 除法律要求或征得您单独同意外，不向他人提供个人信息。

六、未成年人

未成年人请在监护人指导下使用。若发现未经监护人同意收集未成年人信息，我们将依法处理。

七、您的权利

  • 通过应用内功能或卸载等方式管理本地数据；
  • 撤回权限同意；
  • 通过下方联系方式咨询、投诉。

八、政策更新

我们可能修订本政策，并在本页更新日期。重大变更将尽力提示。

Beself Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026 · Effective: April 12, 2026

Welcome to Beself (“the App”). We respect your privacy and take reasonable measures to protect personal information in accordance with applicable laws. Please read this Policy before you install or use the App.

1. Scope

This Policy describes how we handle personal information when you install and use the App on devices such as Android or Windows. The App provides web browsing and on-device utilities. Third-party websites or services you choose to visit are governed by their own policies.

2. Information we process

  • Device and network status: Used for connectivity, downloads, and basic stability (e.g., whether the network is available), not to identify you by itself.
  • On-device app data: Settings, bookmarks-like data, caches, notes, and files you export are stored locally in app-specific storage; we do not upload this to our own servers unless you voluntarily share or upload it elsewhere.
  • Camera, microphone, location: Used only when the system grants permission and a webpage or feature legitimately requests it; you can revoke access in system settings.
  • Notifications: For download completion, foreground task status, etc.; configurable in system settings.
  • Files and media: Accessed through the system picker or paths you explicitly authorize; we do not scan unrelated files beyond your actions.

3. Permissions (as shown by your OS)

  • Network access and state: load pages, downloads, connectivity checks.
  • Foreground services: compliant background sync or media-related tasks with status visibility.
  • Camera, audio recording, audio settings, wake lock: multimedia scenarios in webpages or the App.
  • Storage and media: save and read app data and files you choose; scoped storage and data minimization where applicable.
  • Location: when needed by a webpage or feature and authorized by you.
  • Vibration: light UI feedback.

4. Third parties and content

Third-party sites may collect information independently. Please read their policies. We are not responsible for third-party practices; the App follows common platform security practices (e.g., preferring encrypted transport where possible).

5. Storage and security

  • Data is primarily stored on your device; keep your device secure.
  • We apply reasonable safeguards against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.
  • We do not sell or share personal information with others except where required by law or with your separate consent.

6. Minors

Minors should use the App under guardian guidance. If we learn we collected a minor’s information without appropriate consent, we will take compliant remedial steps.

7. Your rights

  • Manage local data via in-app features or by uninstalling;
  • Withdraw consent for permissions;
  • Contact us using the details below.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy and revise the “Last updated” date. Material changes will be communicated where practicable.
